ABOUT

I'm a creative technologist and Design & Technology student at Parsons School of Design. My work pulls from game design, sound, XR, installation, and code—whatever the idea needs.

I've built browser-based synth playgrounds, room-scale sensory installations, pixel puzzle games, TouchDesigner visuals, and Arduino-driven controllers. Currently designing IRL social games at MuchoHangouts and supporting XR at the Parsons XR Center. Open to creative tech, game design, sound, and immersive work.
