TouchDesigner / Installation 2025

Hand-Tracking
Oscillator

Type Audio-Reactive Installation
Tools TouchDesigner, Mediapipe
Medium Real-time Visual & Sound

An audio-reactive installation built in TouchDesigner that turns your hands into the instrument. Using Mediapipe for real-time hand tracking, the patch reads gesture and position to drive a visual and sound oscillator—so what you see and hear is always tied to how you move in space.

How It Works

A camera feed runs through Mediapipe hand landmarks; those values map to oscillator parameters in TouchDesigner—frequency, amplitude, color, and motion in the scene. Small movements nudge the tone; bigger gestures spike visuals and sound. The goal was a tight feedback loop: no mouse, no slider bank—just body as controller.

Experience

Standing in front of the piece, you learn the system by playing with it. The installation rewards exploration and repetition, like tuning an analog synth with your hands. It sits at the intersection of performance, creative code, and sound design—the same threads I pull through game and installation work.
